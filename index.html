FAQ

Genji was handpicked from the many names that have been shortlisted. We chose Genji as it is a Japanese name because it refers to healing and peace.

Genji is a Japanese/shonen “Funko Pop” themed collection of 5,555 3D avatars. Every trait is painstakingly designed and assembled together from scratch by our team of in-house designers in order to ensure maximum compatibility for a visually attractive final product. Avatars that do not meet our vigorous standards are pruned and/or archived- leaving only the best to be made available in the minting supply.

There will be 5,555 unique Genjis available to mint.

Genji games will allow users to enjoy and participate in a variety of games available such as baccarat, blackjack, slots and many more. These games will be primarily be played with Genji Tokens that will be airdropped in the near future.

Genji Merch will be designed, manufactured and distributed by our very own community members! We want to work closely with our supporters and fans to create community-based products. 100% of all proceeds will enter the community wallet.
